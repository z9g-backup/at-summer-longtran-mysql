## 25. Lấy 5 blog và 5 news mới nhất đã active
```sql
(SELECT 
    category_id,
    title,
    view,
    is_active,
    created_at,
    updated_at,
    'blog' AS type
FROM
    blog
WHERE
    is_active = 1
ORDER BY updated_at DESC , created_at DESC
LIMIT 5) UNION (SELECT 
    category_id,
    title,
    view,
    is_active,
    created_at,
    updated_at,
    'news' AS type
FROM
    news
WHERE
    is_active = 1
ORDER BY updated_at DESC , created_at DESC
LIMIT 5);
```
